The beginning

The day was April 8th 2024 and 8 coders were working "quietly" at LaunchCode HQ. So it all started one day. When the sun and the moon met... during business hours. And we decided as a collective to go on an adventure. Some of us were scared, but we were drawn to the energy. It's a "once in a lifetime event"... every few years. We go outside, Matt gives us the glasses, everyone is looking at the sun. I do want to acknowledge... Kyle was doing his own things. Matt pointed out that if you look at the shadows of the leaves. And that once the moon moved past the sun. It was in that moment that we realized that we have forged a brotherhood for life.
